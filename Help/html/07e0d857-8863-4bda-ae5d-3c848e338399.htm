Client Scanning

Instead of manually entering in the MAC addresses of clients, you can instead scan for visible clients and create terminals or named pipes from a list of detected clients.

Scan For Clients

  1. Click File>Detect Clients and a client list will appear. As clients are discovered, they will be populated in this list.

  2. Right click a discovered client to bring up a menu which lets you create a terminal window or named pipe from that client. When clicked, the MAC address of the terminal will be automatically populated.

  3. You can also enable persistence for scanning, which will maintain the currently scanned clients in the list on a refresh and add any newly found clients to the list.
